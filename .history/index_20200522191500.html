About

Who we are

Gundo Social Scheme is a Non-Profit Organisation with Reg no: 085-904-NPO, a provider of food parcels and other social services to the benefit of the Communities.


What we do

We offer food parcels on a weekly basis, monthly basis or at any time when it is convenient for us to do so , depending on the resources and the funding received as donations from people and other Organisations.We interact with different Communities , depending on their needs We have future goals to train women in sewing skills /dress –making and we shall soon embark on Sports development within Communities when normal circumstances arise.

Vision

Gundo Social Scheme‘s vision is to respond comprehensively to threats posed to underprivileged communities throughout the country especially in disadvantaged areas. We render services which uplift their standard of living and gives them a sense of hope. For them to know that we care, we give out food parcels.


Mission

Getting to know them and to build long-term relationships with disadvantaged communities. Operating and aligning social services to relevant legislation and community needs . Contributing to social development .
