About

Who we are

We are Gundo Social Scheme, a non-profit organization with the founding goal being to help to help South Africa in social development of rural areas as well as providing feeding schemes to the impoverished and charitable ventures to those in need.


What we do

We offer social development, feeding schemes, charity events, sport development, the building of clinics, and providing adequate healthcare to all people through either the distribution of medical resources or providing access to the necessary facilities needed for medical purposes.

Vision

Gundo Social Scheme's vision is to respond comprehensively to threats to underprivileged communities throughout South Africa, especially in rural areas. We want to render rural community services which will uplift their standard of living by giving them the knowledge and skills to develop their communities.


Mission

  • Getting to know and building long-term relationships with rural communities.
  • Operating and aligning social services to relevant legislation and community needs.
  • Contributing to rural community social development like clinics, schools etc.
